Welcome to All Dominion Radio

Who we are

All Dominion Radio is an outreach ministry of Strait & Narrow Ministries INC. Strait & Narrow Ministries was birth by the Spirit of God to fulfill the calling on its founder's life. Minister L. Nathaniel Bull has a passion to help and serve the Kingdom of God as God leads him. ADR serves to fulfill the Great Commission according to Matthew 28:18-20 and the mandate to be witnesses of Christ according to Acts 1:8 by providing a means for the Word of God to be shared throughout the world.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is laid out in Matthew 28:19-20
"...GO and make discriples of all the nations,
baptizing them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit.
Teach these new disciples to
obey all the commands I have given you."
and Acts 1:8
"...and you will be my witnessess in Jerusalem,
and in all Judea and Samaria,
and to the ends of the earth."
